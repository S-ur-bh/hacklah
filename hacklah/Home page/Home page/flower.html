what's JobGuru?

We are a specialized job search website dedicated to connecting individuals with limited education to meaningful job opportunities in fields such as house help, retail shops, construction work, and more. Our mission is to empower those who may not have formal education but possess valuable skills and experiences that are in high demand.

We collaborate with employers who value skills, dedication, and work ethic over formal qualifications. From domestic help positions to retail assistants, construction workers, janitors, and more, we cover a diverse array of job categories where education is not the sole determining factor for success.
